My Story

Fitness is for
everyone.

I'm Misha — a certified fitness instructor based in Malaysia with a passion for helping people transform their bodies and their relationship with health. I believe fitness shouldn't be overwhelming, intimidating or exclusive. It should be accessible, enjoyable and built around your real life.

After becoming FEA certified, I started @misha_fitness_official on Instagram to share my knowledge with as many people as possible — and have grown a community of over 7,400 people who are on the same journey. But Instagram posts can only do so much. Real transformation comes from personalised coaching, consistent support and a plan built specifically for you.

That's exactly what I offer. Whether you want to lose fat, build strength or simply feel better in your body — I will listen, I will plan and I will support you every step of the way.

My Philosophy

Fitness should
fit your life —
not take it over.

I've seen too many people burn out on extreme diets and punishing workout plans that aren't built for real human beings. My approach is different — science-backed, sustainable and built around your actual lifestyle.

I want you to finish our time together not just leaner and stronger, but educated. Understanding why your body responds the way it does, how to eat to fuel your goals and how to stay consistent without feeling deprived.

That's what creates results that last.
